Do not confuse confidence with arrogance. 不要把自信和傲慢混淆。

A lot of men make the mistake of trying to display confidence, which comes across to others as arrogance. 很多男人犯了试图展示自信的错误，这在别人看来是傲慢。

What I've learned about arrogant people is that they are usually the most insecure. 我从傲慢的人身上学到的是，他们通常是最不安全的。

This is why they must remind you about how great they are. 这就是为什么他们必须提醒你他们有多么伟大。

They really aren't trying to convince you, but rather themselves. 他们真的不是在试图说服你，而是在说服他们自己。

Confidence is something that's just displayed- no words need to be spoken. 自信是一种仅仅表现出来的东西——不需要说任何话。

Arrogance is when you verbally let others know of your greatness. 傲慢是当你口头上让别人知道你的伟大时。
