【装逼和自信的区别，吹嘘】

One of the biggest mistakes men make when going on dates is trying to impress women with their accomplishments and success.

男人在约会时犯的最大错误之一就是试图用他们的成就和成功给女人留下深刻印象。

Yes, women prefer a successful man but it's null and void if you're bragging about it.

是的,女人更喜欢成功的男人,但如果你吹嘘这些,那就毫无意义了。

You're pretty much saying "hey, I'm not good enough to attract you for the person I am so let me tell you all these wonderful things I've accomplished."

你基本上是在说:"嘿,我这个人不够好吸引你,所以让我告诉你我所有这些了不起的成就。"

That's a turn oﬀ because you're pretty much admitting that you're of lower value than the woman.

这很扫兴,因为你基本上是在承认你的价值低于这个女人。

Never forget women date up not down.

永远不要忘记女人只会往上约会,不会往下约会。

Always carry yourself as a high-value man.

始终表现得像个高价值的男人。

Never discuss the other women you're dating trying to show that you're a pre-selected.

永远不要讨论你正在约会的其他女人,试图表现你是个抢手货。

It'll only lower her attraction.

这只会降低她对你的吸引力。
